Get started

Quick guides

01

Add your first medication

Tap + on the home screen, search or type your medication name, fill in dosage and schedule.

02

Set up reminders

Open any medication → tap the bell icon → choose time and repeat frequency.

03

Log a dose

Tap the checkmark next to a medication from your home screen or from the reminder notification.

04

Add a family member

Tap Collections → + New Collection → enter a name and start adding their medications.

05

Use AI guidance

Open any medication → tap "Ask AI" to get general information and guidance (Pro feature).

06

Track inventory

Add pill count when creating a medication. 3TABZ will alert you when you're running low.
